About Us

Every journey has a beginning. The one we embarked on starts in 2013 when ImaginXP came into being with a vision to contribute towards the development of the User Experience ecosystem in India. As the country was going through a digital revolution, the founders of the company realized that the habitat of UX design also needs to grow to create world-class digital products. As the digital revolution was at its peak, these visionary folks wanted to contribute to the country and its economy by providing well trained and efficient UX designers to the market. Thus, began the journey of ImaginXP - a Virtual University Platform for Future Skills in the country.
